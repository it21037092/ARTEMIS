Welcome to ARTEMIS – Immersive Artillery Fire Training and Evaluation System

Welcome to the official website for ARTEMIS, a cutting-edge immersive simulation project designed to revolutionize artillery fire training.

ARTEMIS combines VR, AI simulation, and ballistics modeling to create realistic training environments and performance metrics for military personnel.

Project Scope

ARTEMIS helps artillery personnel enhance firing efficiency through interactive, immersive VR environments and intelligent evaluation systems.

About ARTEMIS

ARTEMIS provides a dynamic training experience using VR and real-time ballistic AI. Key components include:

  • Gun End (VR): Control gun elevation/traverse and calculate ToF under simulated weather.
  • Target End: AI evaluates accuracy and performance.
